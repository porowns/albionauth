Drama

Krypted Gaming has a zero tolerance policy on drama. If you stir up something with someone, you'll get booted. At the end of the day, we want to come home and play games.

Respect

There is no reason to be toxic on the internet, especially in our community. Treat your fellow members with respect, especially leadership. Respect the chain of command.

Loot

Loot is a pretty nightmarish issue to handle in Albion, since there is no way to implement master loot. All loot is either sold and donated to the guild treasury, or given to guild members (and then donate a little bit of cost to the treasury). If you have an officer in the party, the officer is the only one that should be looting bodies. This will make it a lot easier in the future.

Activity

Every member in Krypted Gaming is actively playing games. We respect RL first, but the only way to achieve an active playerbase is to enforce activity requirements. At the moment, we have no requirements for Albion Online. Examples for other games include EVE Online, where we require two fleets a week.
